OUR VISION
You are probably here because we agree that Virtual Reality holds big potential for the future. We think VR has a huge opportunity in almost all working spaces. It can help to improve living standards, experience more intens and connecting situations and make you feel happier. In the near future VR will be widely addopted on business scale and also be in most households. Actually VR is already in most households in the form of a smartphone. With your smartphone you can access Web-VR. The VRworks framework is build around Web-VR, so it works on every VR Device.

INNOVATIVE TECHNOLOGY

We have introduced an app that let you practically start all the available content on your VR device, without the hassle of complicated setups. It can be used on your location within your own Wifi Network or you can make use of the build-in Bluetooth connection, so it can be used anywhere your would like!
